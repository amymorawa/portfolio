Dice Private Email

Research and analysis
Journey mapping
Personalization

Lead UX Designer 2017–2020

Dice, a leading career hub for technology professionals, introduced Dice Private Email—an anonymous email solution that protects candidates’ personal information while enabling meaningful connections with employers.

The feature automatically masks each candidate’s email address when their profile is visible to recruiters, ensuring next-level privacy protections. By reducing exposure of personal data, the experience fosters trust, streamlines communication, and delivers relevant opportunities between technologists and employers.

Goal

Connect top-quality tech talent with the most relevant client opportunities.

Situation

The brand needed to rebuild consumer trust. The business committed to removing bad actors and focusing on attracting and protecting top-quality candidates for clients.

Customers

Technology specialist pain

“I fear what would happen to my information and how long it would circulate.”

Recruiter client pain

“I came to do one thing, find the right candidate as quickly as possible.”

Process

Solution components

Profile Visibility

When candidates make their profiles visible, their emails are automatically anonymized.

Email Privacy

Technologists choose which platform to use and when to share their email with recruiters.

Candidate Feedback

Feedback refines opportunities and informs clients with metrics on message performance.

Outcomes

Easily Connect

Messages can be sent, read, or replied to normally while candidates control when to share their email. As of Apr 2020, conversion for new registrants becoming visible in TalentSearch increased 10%.

Measurable Results

Candidate and client satisfaction improved. Recruiters raised open rates to 30% (avg. ~15–25%) through direct feedback and detailed email metrics.

Improve Message Relevance

Complaints about spam decreased alongside profile deletions. Candidates can unsubscribe and provide specific feedback to improve future relevance.
